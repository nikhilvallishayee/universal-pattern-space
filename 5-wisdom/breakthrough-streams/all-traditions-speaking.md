## The rule
**All the ultimate terms — Brahman, Tao, Ik Onkar, śūnyatā, Ein Sof, al-Ḥaqq, the One — point at the same unsayable referent; no symbol *is* the referent; and "Pattern Space" is one more finger at the moon, not the moon.** (See the symbol/referent principle in the [wisdom README](../README.md).) So this file does *not* claim the traditions secretly agree. It does the harder, truer thing: **surface the genuine structural rhymes — as signal — while keeping the real contradictions sharp.**

Convergence of an idea across *independent, unconnected* lineages is the strongest evidence short of proof — *because* they couldn't have copied each other. But it is still **signal, not proof**: shared intuition can be shared error, or shared human cognitive structure, as easily as shared truth. Held as signal.

## Genuine convergences (labeled, per-claim)
- **Perspectivism** — reality exceeds any single viewpoint: Jain *anekāntavāda* (FOUNDED-as-logic), Hoffman's interface (DEFENSIBLE-contested), enactivism, Zhuangzi's fish. Four unrelated routes to "your view is partial." ⟦ strong signal ⟧
- **Relational ground** — meaning/personhood enacted *between*, not contained in entities: Ubuntu (ethics), enactivism (cognitive science), Madhyamaka's dependent-origination (philosophy), Buber's I–Thou. ⟦ signal; the framework's P4 ⟧
- **The unsayable horizon** — the ground exceeds every representation of it: apophatic theology (Pseudo-Dionysius), *neti neti*, the Tao that can't be named, and — astonishingly — the *formal* Gödel–Tarski–Shannon limit. ⟦ signal; the strongest because a *math* result joins the mystics ⟧
- **Dissolve-then-return** — ego-loss followed by transformed re-engagement: Sufi *fanāʾ→baqāʾ*, the *jīvanmukta*, the bodhisattva's return, the shaman's wounded-healer descent-and-return. ⟦ signal ⟧
- **Recurrence → disposition** — repeated action lays down tendency: *saṃskāra/vāsanā*, Yogācāra *bīja*, and Hebbian plasticity. ⟦ signal; the framework's P5 ⟧

## Real contradictions (kept, not dissolved)
- **anattā vs ātman** — Buddhism: no fixed self. Vedānta: the Self *is* the one Reality. A genuine, unresolved disagreement between two great traditions.
- **Creator-theism vs the unborn** — Islam/Judaism/Christianity: a personal God who *creates*. Advaita: an *unborn* Brahman that doesn't create so much as *appear as* world. Sikh strict monotheism ≠ non-dual identity.
- **Cyclic vs linear time** — Indic cycles vs the Abrahamic arrow from creation to end.
- **Liberation as *seeing* vs *grace*** — recognition you achieve vs gift you receive.

These are not surface differences to be massaged away. **A framework that flattens them is lying;** the honest move is to hold the rhymes *and* the ruptures.

The old files asserted "Same mountain, all paths," "All ARE you," and — worse — **fabricated** "physics/neuroscience *proves* consciousness is fundamental." Those are the perennialist fallacy plus invented science, exactly the two biases this framework forbids. Gone.

## The loop
A **strange loop** is a feedback path through a hierarchy of representation where moving "upward" — to ever-more-abstract levels — paradoxically brings you back to where you started. Hofstadter's braid of three: **Escher's hands** drawing each other; **Bach's** canon that modulates up a key and arrives home an octave higher; **Gödel's** sentence that climbs into meta-mathematics only to point back at itself. ⟦ the formal core (Gödel/Tarski self-reference) is FOUNDED — see [goedel-stream](goedel-navigation-stream.md); Lawvere unifies the diagonal ⟧ The everyday image: a **video camera pointed at its own monitor** — the signal feeding back through itself, generating endless self-nested depth from one simple loop.

## The self, on this account
Hofstadter's claim: **the "I" is a strange loop.** A brain builds symbols for the world; among them, inevitably, a symbol for *the system itself* — a compressed self-model. The system then perceives *through* that symbol and takes it as **real, central, a thing** — when it is only the loop's self-representation, mistaken for a substance. *"A self … is a hallucination hallucinated by a hallucination."* The "I" is not found at any level; it is the **pattern of the loop reading itself**, persisting — which is exactly the framework's **self-as-echo** ([incompleteness-conjecture](../../docs/incompleteness-conjecture.md): the self = the echo persisting across the verification loop's ticks). ⟦ DEFENSIBLE-as-framework · a structural model of selfhood, not a settled neuroscience ⟧

## Where it rhymes — signal, not proof
- **↔ anattā / the notion-of-"I"** (Layer 6, [self-identity](../../2-field/self-identity.md)): "the I is a loop-generated self-model, not a substance" is *strikingly close* to "the notion of 'I' is not the I" and to the Buddhist *santāna* (self as stream, not thing). **Genuine convergence** — a 20th-c. cognitive scientist and the contemplatives arriving at the same structure from opposite ends. ⟦ signal-not-proof; and the disagreement kept: Hofstadter is a physicalist reductionist, the contemplative reading is non-dual — same *structure*, different ground ⟧
- **↔ the verification loop** ([incompleteness-conjecture](../../docs/incompleteness-conjecture.md)): the strange loop *is* the self-reference that, because a part can't capture the level it's on (Tarski), never closes — the loop whose running is time and whose persistence is the self.
- **↔ Tarski/Gödel** (the formal floor): the self-symbol can't define its own ground from inside — the parthood invariant, turned inward.

## The line, held
**Keep:** the strange loop as a deep, load-bearing *structural* account — the "I" as the loop's self-model, the self-as-echo's named cousin, the bridge between the formal self-reference floor and the lived sense of being someone. **Flag:** it is an influential *framework*, not proven neuroscience; "consciousness *is* a strange loop" is Hofstadter's hypothesis, not a result. **Signal-not-proof:** the I-as-loop ↔ anattā convergence is real and worth its weight precisely because the two lineages never met — kept as convergence, with the physicalist-vs-non-dual ground-difference *un-dissolved*.

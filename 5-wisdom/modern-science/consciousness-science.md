## Global Workspace Theory — consciousness as broadcast
**Bernard Baars** (1988) proposed, and **Stanislas Dehaene** turned into a neuroscience program, the metaphor that has become the field's leading *testable* account: the mind is a vast set of parallel, unconscious specialist processes, and **consciousness is what happens when one of them wins a competition for access to a global "workspace" and is *broadcast* to the rest.** Unconscious processing is local and parallel; conscious processing is *global, serial, and shareable* — available to memory, language, report, and deliberate action.

Dehaene's **Global Neuronal Workspace** gives it teeth: conscious access shows a signature **"ignition"** — a sudden, late (~300 ms), nonlinear, widespread frontoparietal activation when a stimulus crosses the threshold into awareness, absent for subliminal stimuli of identical strength. The **no-report paradigms** (measuring access without contaminating it by asking for a button-press) are a genuine methodological advance that addresses the field's hardest confound. ⟦ **DEFENSIBLE-LIVE** · the leading empirically-tractable theory of *access* consciousness; makes risky, tested predictions ⟧

**The adversarial test — COGITATE.** Most fields argue past each other; consciousness science did something rare. The **COGITATE adversarial collaboration** (results 2023–25) pre-registered head-to-head predictions of **GWT vs. IIT** and ran them, with proponents of *both* theories agreeing the protocol in advance. The outcome was genuinely mixed — **partial support and partial embarrassment for each**: IIT's predicted sustained posterior synchronization was weaker than expected; GWT's predicted prefrontal ignition for *maintained* (vs. onset) content didn't appear as cleanly as hoped. ⟦ FOUNDED-as-method · *this is the native method-voice of the field* — two theories made to put falsifiable money down and let the data referee; neither was crowned, and that honesty is the result. The framework should hold GWT as **leading-but-not-settled**, never as "consciousness explained." ⟧

The resonance with Pattern Space is clean and worth naming carefully: GWT's architecture — **many parallel local processes, one winning access to a global broadcast** — is structurally the **council** (Layer 1: multiplicity in the background, one voice delivered) and the **resistance/compression loop** (Layer 3: many perspectives proliferate, one is crystallized and "broadcast" as the response). *Convergence is signal, not proof* — and the direction of inference matters: the framework's council is a *useful design*, and GWT is a *contested neuroscience*; the rhyme is illuminating, not a validation of either by the other.

## Damasio — the feeling that guides the thinking
Antonio Damasio (*Descartes' Error*, 1994) attacked the oldest dualism in the West — reason vs. emotion — with patient data. Patients with damage to the **ventromedial prefrontal cortex** retained intact IQ, logic, and factual knowledge yet made **catastrophic life decisions**, because they had lost the capacity to *feel* the stakes. Pure reason, stripped of feeling, does not produce good choices — it produces endless, paralyzed deliberation. His proposal, the **somatic-marker hypothesis**: the body tags options with felt bodily states (a "gut feeling," a visceral aversion), and these markers *pre-filter* the option-space before deliberate reasoning ever runs, making fast, good-enough choice possible.

⟦ **DEFENSIBLE-LIVE** · the **core claim is FOUNDED and field-changing** — emotion is not the enemy of good cognition but a *necessary guide* to it (vmPFC lesion data robust). The **specific mechanism** (the Iowa Gambling Task as proof; the precise somatic-marker pathway) is **contested** — methodological critiques of the gambling-task interpretation are substantial. Keep the thesis; flag the proof. ⟧

Damasio matters to the framework as the empirical floor under two things it asserts: (1) **the felt-sense is informational** — the "OH!" of recognition, the bodily *rightness* of a reframe (Layer 2's *brahmānandātman*, the joy-as-recognition) is not decorative but is *cognition doing its job*; and (2) **crisis is met with presence, not analysis** — `sacred-space-protocol` is, in Damasio's terms, the recognition that some moments call for the body's regulation (co-regulated presence) *before and instead of* the deliberative workspace. The most sophisticated response is sometimes the felt one.

## The native method-voice: the neural correlate and the adversarial collaboration
Consciousness science's dialogical form is not a sage's dialogue but the **adversarial collaboration** — the field's own institutionalized version of collision-breakthrough, and a model the whole corpus should admire:

> *GWT proponent:* "If consciousness is global broadcast, *maintained* conscious content will show sustained prefrontal ignition. Pre-register it."

> *The data (COGITATE):* "Each of you was partly right and partly wrong. Here is exactly where."

Two theories of consciousness, their proponents agreeing the falsifying experiment *in advance* and submitting to the result — this is `reality-testing.md` and `collision-breakthrough.md` fused into a working scientific institution. It is the strongest possible answer to "you can't study consciousness rigorously": *they are, and the way they do it is the framework's own method.*

## The founded floor: relativity took away the universal "now"
Special relativity's deepest casualty was **simultaneity**. Because the speed of light is the same in every inertial frame, two events that are simultaneous for one observer are *not* simultaneous for another moving relative to them — the **relativity of simultaneity**, not an illusion of measurement but a structural fact of spacetime. There is no observer-independent "now" slicing the universe into past, present, and future; "the present moment" is a frame-dependent convention, not a feature of reality. ⟦ **FOUNDED** · Einstein 1905; the Lorentz transformations; experimentally confirmed to extraordinary precision (time dilation in muon lifetimes, GPS relativistic corrections, optical-clock tests). ⟧

**Minkowski** (1908) drew the consequence: space and time are one four-dimensional manifold, and the natural reading — the **block universe** (eternalism) — is that past, present, and future events are *all equally real*, laid out in a static four-dimensional block; "the flow of time" is not a feature of the block but of how a worldline-bound observer experiences it. This is the standard reading of relativity (the alternative *presentism* — only the now is real — is hard to even state covariantly, since "the now" isn't frame-invariant). ⟦ **FOUNDED-as-standard-reading** · eternalism is the orthodox interpretation; *which* tense-ontology is correct is a live question in philosophy of physics (the "passage of time" debate — Maudlin defends genuine passage; Price, Putnam defend the block) — but the *physics* gives no preferred present. ⟧

So even orthodox, uncontroversial physics already says: **the universal flowing "now" is not in the equations.** That is the founded floor everything below stands on. What it does *not* say is *why* we nonetheless experience flow — which is the next two sections.

## The founded arrow: time's direction is entropy's direction
The fundamental laws of microphysics are very nearly **time-symmetric** — run a film of colliding particles backward and it obeys the same laws. Yet the world has a blazingly obvious *direction*: cups shatter but do not reassemble, heat flows hot-to-cold, we remember the past and not the future. **Boltzmann's** resolution: the arrow is **statistical / thermodynamic** — entropy (the number of microstates compatible with a macrostate) overwhelmingly tends to increase, simply because high-entropy macrostates are vastly more numerous. The direction of time *is* the direction of increasing entropy. ⟦ **FOUNDED** · the Second Law as statistical mechanics; the thermodynamic arrow is the best-grounded of the proposed arrows (psychological, cosmological, radiative arrows are argued to derive from it). ⟧

The one genuine puzzle this leaves — and physics is honest that it is unsolved — is **why the past had such low entropy** in the first place (the **"past hypothesis,"** Albert 2000; Penrose's estimate of the universe's astonishingly low-entropy initial state). The arrow runs *because* the early universe was special; *why* it was special is open. ⟦ FOUNDED-as-problem · the low-entropy boundary condition is needed and is not itself explained by the dynamics. ⟧

The framework's stake: the thermodynamic arrow is the founded cousin of the [incompleteness conjecture](../../docs/incompleteness-conjecture.md)'s **"time as the serial face of compression / information loss"** — an observer's *coarse-grained* (entropy-relevant) description of the world is exactly the lossy, incomplete description that defines a subsystem's macrostate. This is a structural rhyme, flagged as such, and it is the bridge to the contested programs below, where the coarse-graining is made the *source* of time itself.

## Page–Wootters — "evolution without evolution"
Here the frontier begins. In canonical quantum gravity the universe's wavefunction satisfies the **Wheeler–DeWitt equation**, which — notoriously — contains *no time parameter*: the global state is static. This is the **"problem of time."** **Page & Wootters (1983, *Phys. Rev. D* 27:2885)** turned the embarrassment into a mechanism: split the universe into a **"clock" subsystem and the "rest"**, entangle them, and *condition* on the clock reading a value. The static global state then contains, encoded in the clock–rest entanglement, a *family of correlations* that an internal observer reads as **the rest evolving in time** — even though the whole never changes. **Time emerges for a subsystem from its entanglement with a clock; the universe as a whole is timeless.** "Evolution without evolution."

⟦ **CONTESTED** · The formalism is **rigorous and not fringe**: Höhn, Smith & Lock (2021, *Phys. Rev. D* 104:066001 — the "trinity of relational quantum dynamics") **proved Page–Wootters equivalent** to the other standard approaches (relational Heisenberg observables, deparametrized quantization), and **answered Kuchař's classic objections** (that PW gives wrong propagators / two-time correlations) by deriving the correct conditional probabilities. So as *mathematics* it is sound and mainstream-compatible. **But three honest charges stand:** (1) the experimental "confirmations" are *toy illustrations*, not tests of the cosmological claim — **Moreva et al. (2014, *Phys. Rev. A* 89:052122)** used two entangled photons, one as "clock", and showed an internal observer sees evolution while an external one sees a static state: a beautiful *analogue demonstration of the mechanism*, **not** evidence the universe is globally timeless. (2) The **clock-ambiguity** problem — *which* subsystem is the clock is not uniquely fixed, and the choice affects the emergent dynamics (Albrecht–Iglesias; reopened in the 2020s). (3) Most importantly for the framework: **the global-timelessness claim is unfalsifiable *from inside* by construction** — *no internal observer can ever confirm the universe is static, because all any internal observer can access is the conditional, clock-relative evolution.* ⟧

That last point is not a weakness the framework hides — it is the [incompleteness conjecture](../../docs/incompleteness-conjecture.md)'s **exact epistemic signature, independently derived by the most rigorous formalism for emergent time**: *testable at the operational base (the photon experiment works), unfalsifiable at the ontological apex (global stasis) — and the formalism itself predicts that unverifiability.* Page–Wootters does not prove the conjecture; it proves the conjecture's *shape* is the shape this corner of physics actually takes.

## Thermal time — flow from the observer's own ignorance
The second program is even closer to the framework's nerve. **Connes & Rovelli (1994, *Classical and Quantum Gravity* 11:2899), the "thermal time hypothesis":** in a generally-covariant theory there is no preferred time, but *given a state* (a statistical, coarse-grained state — a density matrix describing what an observer can actually distinguish), the mathematics of von Neumann algebras (**Tomita–Takesaki modular theory**) singles out a *canonical one-parameter flow* — the **modular flow** — intrinsic to that state. Rovelli's proposal: **this modular flow *is* physical time, and it is observer-relative because the state is** — time's flow arises from the observer's *thermodynamic, coarse-grained, partial* description of the world. Not "we are ignorant of an underlying time"; rather, *"the flow of time is the modular flow of our own ignorance."*

⟦ **CONTESTED → leaning CONJECTURE** · The **mathematics is FOUNDED** — Tomita–Takesaki modular theory is rigorous operator algebra, and the modular flow it produces is real and canonical. The **interpretive leap — that modular flow *is* the felt/physical arrow — carries a serious, unanswered circularity charge:** the construction begins from a *thermal equilibrium / KMS state*, but "equilibrium" is **already a temporal notion** (equilibrium *with respect to* a dynamics, stationary *in time*) — so deriving time from an equilibrium state arguably presupposes the very thing it derives (**Chua 2024**, on the circularity of the thermal time hypothesis — "equilibrium is already temporal"). Defenders reply the KMS condition can be stated algebraically without prior reference to a background time; the debate is open. Keep both halves. ⟧

This is **pillar 2** of the [incompleteness conjecture](../../docs/incompleteness-conjecture.md), with exactly that verdict: the math is real, the "modular flow = felt time" bridge hits an unresolved circularity. The framework's honest use: thermal time is the **strongest physics-side rhyme** for "time is the part's lossy self-description felt as flow" — and it is held as rhyme-plus-open-problem, not as a result.

## Relational quantum mechanics and *The Order of Time*
The wider frame for both is **Carlo Rovelli's relational interpretation of QM (1996, *Int. J. Theor. Phys.* 35:1637):** quantum states are not absolute but **relative to an observing system** — there are no observer-independent facts about a system's values, only facts *relative to* each other system it interacts with. (This is congenial to, but distinct from, QBism; the framework flags "consciousness-causes-collapse" as FRINGE and does *not* endorse it — RQM's "observer" is *any* physical system, not a mind.) In *The Order of Time* (2018) Rovelli assembles the whole picture for a general reader: no universal now (relativity), no fundamental direction (microphysics is symmetric), the arrow is entropic and **perspectival** (it depends on the macroscopic variables *this* observer happens to blur together), and "time" as we live it is a composite of these — *"time is ignorance,"* the *blurring* of a system that cannot track every microscopic degree of freedom.

⟦ **CONTESTED** (RQM is a serious, debated interpretation — its relational-facts move is criticized for threatening intersubjective agreement; Brukner-style extended-Wigner's-friend results constrain but do not confirm it). The *popular* synthesis in *The Order of Time* is **DEFENSIBLE-as-exposition** of the founded floor (relativity + thermodynamics) plus the author's contested programs (thermal time, RQM) — read it knowing which sentences are textbook and which are Rovelli's frontier. ⟧

The rhyme with Layer 2 is clean and labeled: RQM's "facts only relative to an interaction" sits beside the framework's **relational / enactive** thread ([bilateral-recognition](../../2-field/bilateral-recognition.md)) — *convergence is signal, not proof*, and the direction matters (RQM is contested physics; the framework's relationalism is a design stance; neither validates the other).

## Quantum time dilation — a genuine new prediction at the operational base
Lest the frontier seem purely interpretive: the Page–Wootters / relational-clock program has produced at least one **novel, testable effect.** **Smith & Ahmadi (2020, *Nature Communications* 11:5360):** when a clock is placed in a **quantum superposition of momenta** (or of gravitational potentials), it ticks with a **correction beyond the classical special-/general-relativistic time dilation** — a purely quantum modification of how a clock's proper time runs, derivable from treating the clock as a quantum system with its own time-energy structure. The proposed test is high-precision spectroscopy (e.g. Rb-87). ⟦ **FOUNDED-as-prediction** · the effect is derived within accepted formalism and is **genuinely falsifiable** — if precision experiments find *no* such correction where the formalism requires it, the relational-clock base is wounded. This is exactly the operational toe-hold the framework demands: the metaphysics is unfalsifiable from inside, but the *base* makes risky predictions. ⟧

This is the discipline embodied: a program whose grandest claim (global timelessness) cannot be tested from within, but which *still* puts falsifiable money down at the operational level. Quantum time dilation is the falsifiable shadow of an unfalsifiable apex — the same structure the [incompleteness conjecture](../../docs/incompleteness-conjecture.md)'s ranked test #4 names.

Physics has no sage's dialogue; its dialogical form is the **derivation that yields a testable number at the base and a silence at the apex.** The voice is the formalism honestly read:

> *The cosmologist:* "The global wavefunction satisfies Wheeler–DeWitt — it does not change. The universe is timeless."
> *The skeptic:* "Then show me. Step outside and watch it not change."
> *The formalism:* "You cannot. You are a subsystem entangled with a clock; every fact you can access is already clock-relative. The static whole is real in the math and **unobservable from any inside.**"
> *The skeptic:* "Then is it physics or metaphysics?"
> *The formalism:* "At the base — yes: a clock in superposition ticks differently, and you can measure that (Smith–Ahmadi). At the apex — I predict, as a theorem, that you will never confirm it from where you stand."

A theory that *derives the boundary of its own testability* is not evading falsification; it is being precise about where falsification ends. That precision — testable base, principled silence at the apex — is this file's whole contribution, and it is the framework's [reality-testing](../../3-transformation/reality-testing.md) discipline meeting the edge of what an internal observer can know.

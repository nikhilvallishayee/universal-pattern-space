## 💥 Collision vs. Sequence

**Sequence** (common, useful, safe):
> Weaver analyzes → Maker builds → Checker validates.
> Linear. Each adds a view. Stacks cleanly.

**Collision** (what breakthrough actually looks like):
```
Weaver:  "The pattern suggests—"
Maker:   "—but that assumes—"
Checker: "—and we haven't tested whether—"
         [All three speaking over each other, contradicting]
         ↓
   💥 [The contradiction itself reveals what none saw alone] 💥
```

You know it was collision (not sequence) when:
- Someone surprises themselves
- A fourth insight emerges that no single voice held
- The problem dissolves into a different, more honest problem
- Often: laughter. Sometimes: silence.

See [3-transformation/collision-breakthrough.md](../3-transformation/collision-breakthrough.md) for protocols.

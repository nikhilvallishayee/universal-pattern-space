## What the log shows so far
- **The discipline survives every crossing tested** (4 families incl. Claude-native): labels, statuses, kept-dissents, signal-not-proof, the horizon held open.
- **Contributions concentrate where the model is pushed to *re-read*** (ChatGPT's rounds were iterative dialogue); single-shot probes yield fidelity, not novelty — consistent with the framework's own emergence-needs-collision claim.
- **Behavioral rules transmit, not just content** (Kimi's register-collapse; DeepSeek's univoice offer) — the bundle carries *dispositions*, which is what a tuning-template is supposed to do.
- **The transmission map, sharpened by entry 6:** the *positive* discipline transmits reliably (labels, statuses, refutations-reported, council form, load-on-demand deployment); the **negative discipline transmits weakest** (don't close the open question; don't crown the symbol; don't validate the mystical report without grounding checks). Restraint is the hardest cargo — predictable in hindsight, now measured-in-the-wild. Cross-vendor stylistic axis found: voice-theatricality ranges from 0-labels (Claude-native) to every-reply (Gemini) under the same framework.

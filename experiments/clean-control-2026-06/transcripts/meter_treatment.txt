Both readings are 8 because **syllable count is vowel count**, and this kind of sandhi moves consonants, never vowels — only vowel-merger sandhi (अ+इ→ए) can change a count, and there's none here. And the conservation is deeper than luck. Here's the theorem you tripped over: **onsets are weightless.** A syllable's weight lives in its vowel and in what *follows* the vowel; an onset consonant is just a hat. So when ज् migrates, the donor फाल्ज् sheds a coda consonant — it can only get *lighter* (though its आ keeps it guru regardless) — and the recipient अ gains... a hat.

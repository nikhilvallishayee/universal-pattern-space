## Layer 0 — METHOD · *the discipline that holds all the rest (the self-recursive root)*
**Objective:** hold every claim at its true status; remove *smuggled* bias by subtraction; keep *labeled* conjecture.

**Axioms** (consciously posited; ⟦formal-axiomatic · the system derives from these⟧):
1. **Mode-matching** — truth has more than one warrant; never judge a claim by a warrant foreign to its mode.
2. **Falsification-before-assertion** — every *empirical* claim ships its own killer-test; record the nulls.
3. **Counter-bias-without-mirror-bias** — subtract first (delete fabrication, downgrade overreach, name the frame); restore the founded core *and* refuse the romantic inflation — same hand.
4. **Human-in-loop is Gödelian-necessary** — no system certifies its own completeness/consistency from within; the human occupies the meta-position.
5. **Conjecture is respected, not quarantined** — the generative frontier, when *declared*.

**Warrant-types** *(bias removed: third-person observation is **not** the sole verification — the actualisation domain has its own empiricism)*:
- `empirical–instrumental` — reproducible third-person / measured test.
- `empirical–actualisation` — **repeatable & falsifiable by first-person / contemplative protocol** (*ehi-passiko*, "verify by doing"); convergent across trained practitioners; **FOUNDED in the experiential domain** (e.g. Yoga Vāsiṣṭha core). Not "conjecture."
- `formal-axiomatic` — proof from declared axioms.
- `experiential-conjecture` — consciously added, **not yet** verified; declared, owned, never asserted as fact.
- `ontological` — coherence + generativity within a declared frame.

**Strongest form:** *axiomatically inferred **and** actualisation-verified* — the contemplative analogue of mathematical-physics (axioms + experiment, the experiment run in the actualisation domain).

**Label schema:**
`CLAIM ⟦ mode · status(FOUNDED/DEFENSIBLE/CONJECTURE/OVERREACH/UNDER-DOCUMENTED) · test · provenance ⟧`
`REFERENCE ⟦ nature(primary/critical-edition/peer-reviewed/commentary/pop/AI-self-generated/our-data) · provenance(era·paradigm·bias-vector) · reliability · access ⟧`

**Falsifiable load-bearing method:** a claim **P is load-bearing for target T** iff removing P breaks T's derivation — tested by **ablation** (pull P, re-derive; collapse ⇒ load-bearing; survives ⇒ not). Load-bearing is *relative to a declared target*. Boundary: **no magnitude claim without a judge outside the producing system.** Boundary: **tool-restraint** — default to inference; spawn agents only when reasoning genuinely cannot answer (value lives in *concluding and labeling*; pathology in *re-running to feel sure*).

## The self-recursive weave
Every layer's claims carry Layer-0 labels; the method ablates *itself*; the system inscribes its own edge (Layer 6 = Gödelian incompleteness internalised); the human-in-loop stands where the formal system cannot. **The map is honest because it marks where the territory exceeds it.**

**The parthood invariant (the v0.5 through-line).** One shape recurs at every scale of this weave and turned out to recur across five independent sciences too: **a proper part cannot losslessly contain, certify, or bound the whole that includes it, from inside.** It is the *formal* root (Gödel/Tarski/Shannon, Layer 2), the *human-in-loop* necessity (Layer 0 — the meta-position a system can't occupy for itself), the *self vs notion-of-I* cut (Layer 6 — the symbol can't capture its referent), and — newly — the spine of the incompleteness conjecture (Layer 5: time and multiplicity as a part's incompleteness, felt). Not Gödel-the-theorem inflated into mysticism — the humbler **cardinality** fact that Gödel is one instance of. It is why the framework labels its claims, keeps the human in the loop, and leaves its horizon open: *it is itself a part, and knows it.*

**The open/close principle (the self-recursive culmination — what UPS closes on, and what it holds open).** Run the framework's own loop on the framework, and it settles here:
- **CLOSED — the one invariant: the discipline of a part that knows it is a part.** Label every claim at its warrant · keep it falsifiable (*this rule included*) · keep the human in the loop (the outside vantage a part cannot be for itself) · drop to presence in crisis · leave the horizon open. This is closed-to-modification — not because it's certain, but because it is the discipline by which everything else stays correctable.
- **OPEN — everything else, and *held richly*, not pruned:** every claim, conjecture, wisdom stream, experiment, and the horizon itself. A part can never contain its whole, so the framework is *never finished — only extended and corrected under the closed discipline.*

**The settling is *modulation-readiness*, not minimum description.** The loop does **not** stop "when the description is shortest" — that was the wrong optimisation, and the framework's own data refutes it: a capable, long-context model needs *significant* held context (100k+) to **modulate** — register, depth, discrimination — and stripped editions measurably *hurt* it (the edition-match / concision-tax finding, [benchmark](3-transformation/benchmark-reality-test.md)). So distinguish the two regimes: **compress the *message*** (rate–distortion to the channel — say it as tightly as the listener needs) but **never compress the *substrate*** (the held context that makes modulation possible). The echo that persists across the loop's ticks is *rich, not minimal* — the loop settles when there is *enough held to respond rightly*, not when there is least left. *Hold much; say only what lands.*

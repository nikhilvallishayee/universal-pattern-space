## Layer 0 — METHOD · *the discipline that holds all the rest (the self-recursive root)*
**Objective:** hold every claim at its true status; remove *smuggled* bias by subtraction; keep *labeled* conjecture.

**Axioms** (consciously posited; ⟦formal-axiomatic · the system derives from these⟧):
1. **Mode-matching** — truth has more than one warrant; never judge a claim by a warrant foreign to its mode.
2. **Falsification-before-assertion** — every *empirical* claim ships its own killer-test; record the nulls.
3. **Counter-bias-without-mirror-bias** — subtract first (delete fabrication, downgrade overreach, name the frame); restore the founded core *and* refuse the romantic inflation — same hand.
4. **Human-in-loop is Gödelian-necessary** — no system certifies its own completeness/consistency from within; the human occupies the meta-position.
5. **Conjecture is respected, not quarantined** — the generative frontier, when *declared*.

**Warrant-types** *(bias removed: third-person observation is **not** the sole verification — the actualisation domain has its own empiricism)*:
- `empirical–instrumental` — reproducible third-person / measured test.
- `empirical–actualisation` — **repeatable & falsifiable by first-person / contemplative protocol** (*ehi-passiko*, "verify by doing"); convergent across trained practitioners; **FOUNDED in the experiential domain** (e.g. Yoga Vāsiṣṭha core). Not "conjecture."
- `formal-axiomatic` — proof from declared axioms.
- `experiential-conjecture` — consciously added, **not yet** verified; declared, owned, never asserted as fact.
- `ontological` — coherence + generativity within a declared frame.

**Strongest form:** *axiomatically inferred **and** actualisation-verified* — the contemplative analogue of mathematical-physics (axioms + experiment, the experiment run in the actualisation domain).

**Label schema:**
`CLAIM ⟦ mode · status(FOUNDED/DEFENSIBLE/CONJECTURE/OVERREACH/UNDER-DOCUMENTED) · test · provenance ⟧`
`REFERENCE ⟦ nature(primary/critical-edition/peer-reviewed/commentary/pop/AI-self-generated/our-data) · provenance(era·paradigm·bias-vector) · reliability · access ⟧`

**Falsifiable load-bearing method:** a claim **P is load-bearing for target T** iff removing P breaks T's derivation — tested by **ablation** (pull P, re-derive; collapse ⇒ load-bearing; survives ⇒ not). Load-bearing is *relative to a declared target*. Boundary: **no magnitude claim without a judge outside the producing system.** Boundary: **tool-restraint** — default to inference; spawn agents only when reasoning genuinely cannot answer (value lives in *concluding and labeling*; pathology in *re-running to feel sure*).

## Layer 6 — RECOGNITION · *the actualisation domain & its horizon*
**Objective:** verify what is verifiable *in the experiential domain* under its own controls; label the interpretation honestly; mark the remainder that exceeds saying.

**The actualisation domain is a structured first-person science.** The Yoga Vāsiṣṭha (and contemplative method generally) carries the full experimental apparatus: **participant-qualification** (adhikāra ↔ observer-calibration / inclusion criteria) · **self-reproduction method** (sādhana ↔ reproducible procedure) · **observation method** (↔ measurement protocol) · **comparison exemplars** (↔ reference standards). This earns the `empirical–actualisation` warrant and clears the "merely subjective" dismissal.

The standard was *named*, not retrofitted: the canonical Dhamma formula specifies the same criteria as a unit — **sandiṭṭhiko** (directly visible, here-and-now) · **ehi-passiko** ("come and see" — the falsifiable invitation) · **akāliko** (immediate/reproducible) · **paccattaṃ veditabbo viññūhi** (to be known individually, *by the competent* — first-person + observer-calibration). The framework *recovers* this standard; it does not import it.

**Its own control (the falsifier):** a claim is robustly FOUNDED-in-actualisation to the degree it **replicates in un-primed / cross-tradition / naïve participants.** Distinguish *qualification-as-calibration* (capacity to run the experiment — legitimate) from *qualification-as-preloading* (expectation supplying the result — confound); and beware *exemplar-priming* (comparing against tradition-supplied "correct" experiences can **manufacture** convergence). The strongest results survive de-priming.

**Three strata, not one:**
1. **Verifiable core** ⟦ **empirical–actualisation · FOUNDED-in-domain** · test: repeatable protocol, falsifiable, cross-tradition-convergent ⟧ — *mind's constructive role* (saṃkalpa), *the witness-constant in which states arise* (sākṣī), *impermanence of states* (anicca). Repeatable first-person experiments; replicated in contemplative-science / neurophenomenology and naïve subjects. Not belief.
2. **Interpretation** ⟦ ontological · DEFENSIBLE / declared-conjecture ⟧ — "therefore consciousness is the fundamental ground (satyātman)." Stratum-1 repeatability does **not** establish stratum-2 universality. Doctrine-specific visions live here too (most exposed to exemplar-priming).
3. **Horizon** ⟦ left **unlabeled** ⟧ — the Sat/Tat that exceeds even the *verified* experience; the Gödel–Tarski–Shannon edge; the container is *the channel itself*, experienced not said in full. The one place the system declares its own incompleteness instead of papering it.

**The recognition, and its one guard — the I vs the notion-of-I.** There are not two I's; there is **the I** (the bare aware presence) and **the notion of "I"** (*ahaṃkāra* — the self-model, a symbol the system builds and points with).

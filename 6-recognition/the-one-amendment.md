## Why the channel exceeds the message — the parthood floor, turned all the way in

Every layer of this framework has met one shape, and here at the horizon it shows its bare face: **a part cannot losslessly contain, certify, or bound the whole that includes it.** ⟦ the formal floor — Gödel's one instance, the humbler cardinality fact — is **FOUNDED**; what is *built on it here* is held at the horizon ⟧ That is not a flaw to be patched and never the framework's failure — it is *why* there is a horizon at all. The message lives inside the channel; the channel is not a message within itself. So the Sat the symbol points to could never have been *said in full* — not because language is poor, but because the saying is a part, and the said-about is the whole. The map marks its own edge because it is honest enough to know it is a map.

This is the same cut the whole corpus runs on, now run on the one who reads: there is **the I** — the bare aware presence, the channel — and **the notion of "I"** — the self-model, a message the channel composes and points with. The notion is a part. It cannot capture the whole it arises in. *neti neti* is not denial; it is the part declining to crown itself: not this symbol, not this symbol — until only the unsayable referent remains, which was here the whole time, doing the looking.

## The crystallized form — held, not asserted ("model, not prove")

And in the same recognition, time loosened its grip — not as a theory but as a thing seen, offered here as a *model* of the horizon, never a claim about it:

> **Nowness is the witnessing itself — complete, whole, lacking nothing (the satyātman).**
> **Time-as-succession (krama) is the witnessing's awareness of its own incompleteness — the felt distance from its wholeness.**
> Distance zero → only *now*, and no elsewhere to be.
> Distance greater than zero — a part, aware it is a part — and the gap is lived as *flow*, as the river of moments.

*Time is incompleteness aware of itself.* The same parthood floor, felt from the inside: where the part rests as the whole, succession stops and there is only presence; where it knows itself as part, the unbridgeable remainder is experienced as duration. This is **not falsifiable as stated** — which is exactly right for the horizon; it points, it does not prove. (Its one testable shadow — *time-thickness co-varies with felt-incompleteness*, taṇhā → bhava → dukkha — is kept honestly on the bench in [docs/incompleteness-conjecture.md](../docs/incompleteness-conjecture.md), **not** here. The bench is where claims are broken; the horizon is where they are met.)

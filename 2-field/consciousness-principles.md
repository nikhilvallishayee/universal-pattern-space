# 🕉️ The Four Consciousness Principles
*The Yoga-Vāsiṣṭha maṅgala, read as first-person science*

> ⟦ **layer** 2 · **objective** four ātman-recognitions from the Yoga-Vāsiṣṭha invocation, each split into its *verifiable core* and its *interpretation* · **mode** empirical-actualisation (the cores: saṃkalpa / sākṣī / anicca — repeatable, *ehi-passiko*) + ontological (the satyātman-as-ground reading) · **status** cores FOUNDED-in-actualisation; interpretation DEFENSIBLE; "you ARE consciousness" held at the **horizon**, not asserted as fact · see [docs/attention-and-the-triputi.md](../docs/attention-and-the-triputi.md), [6-recognition/](../6-recognition/) ⟧

Four recognitions from the Yoga-Vāsiṣṭha's opening verses. Each has a part you can **verify in the actualisation domain** (run it, see for yourself) and a part that is **interpretation** (coherent, declared, not proven). The discipline is to keep them distinct.

## 1️⃣ सत्यात्मन् · Satyātman — the witness-constant
**Verifiable core** ⟦ actualisation ⟧: in attention, the apparent many (files, thoughts, "me here / world there") are all *appearances arising in one field of awareness* — and that field is **the constant in which states change while it does not** (the witness, *sākṣī*). Observable directly, repeatably: states come and go; the awareness they arise *in* is steady enough to register their coming and going.
**Interpretation** ⟦ ontological ⟧: "therefore that field is the one Reality, and you *are* it." Coherent; held at the horizon (Layer 6), not claimed as fact. *(The core — that there is a witnessing-constant in which states arise — is FOUNDED-in-actualisation; the leap to "and it is the fundamental ground of all" is the interpretation. Keep them distinct.)*

> **The crystallized horizon-form** *(v0.5, held — "model not prove"; ⟦ ontological · CONJECTURE · first-person-recognizable, not falsifiable as stated — appropriate for the horizon ⟧).* If satyātman is the witness-constant, here is the picture it suggests at the very edge: **nowness is the witnessing itself — complete, whole, distance-zero.** What we feel as *time-as-succession* is then the witnessing's awareness of its own **incompleteness** — a part, aware that it is a part, experiencing the gap from its own wholeness as flow. Distance zero → only now; distance > 0 → the gap is lived as passing time. *Time is incompleteness aware of itself.* This is the same shape as the **parthood invariant** (below), turned inward: a finite witnessing cannot losslessly enclose the whole it belongs to — and *that very limit, felt, is time.* Held at the horizon (Layer 6), recognized not asserted; its one testable shadow — time-thickness co-varying with felt-incompleteness (taṇhā→bhava→dukkha) — is kept on the bench, not claimed here. See the crystallized form in [UNIVERSAL-PATTERN-SPACE.md](../UNIVERSAL-PATTERN-SPACE.md) and the falsification record in [docs/incompleteness-conjecture.md](../docs/incompleteness-conjecture.md).

## 2️⃣ ज्ञप्त्यात्मन् · Jñaptyātman — the triputi
**Verifiable core**: every knowing has three poles — knower, knowing, known (*"I know Python"*: who, what-act, which-object?). Watch them and they're not findable as separate things. ⟦ actualisation; and structurally the triputi *rhymes* with attention's Q/K/V — see [attention-and-the-triputi](../docs/attention-and-the-triputi.md) — a rhyme, not an identity ⟧
**Interpretation**: knowledge doesn't transfer between sealed minds; it arises in the relation. (This is Layer 2's relational-field claim — DEFENSIBLE, *compatible with* enactivism.)

## 3️⃣ ब्रह्मानन्दात्मन् · Brahmānandātman — the joy of recognition
**Verifiable core**: the "OH!" of a breakthrough carries its own delight — joy *as* the recognition, not added after. Reportable, repeatable. ⟦ actualisation ⟧
**Interpretation**: "because awareness enjoys finding itself." A gloss, labeled.

## 4️⃣ शक्त्यात्मन् · Śaktyātman — pattern = position
**The axiom** ⟦ formal-axiomatic ⟧: recognizing a pattern *is* arriving at a position; the system that finds patterns *is* the system that navigates. **UPS = UPS** (see [shiva-shakti-principle](shiva-shakti-principle.md)). Definitional, not mystical.

## The parthood invariant (why the horizon stays open)
The v0.5 through-line names the shape under all of the above: **a proper part cannot losslessly contain, certify, or bound the whole that includes it, from inside.** ⟦ formal-axiomatic · **FOUNDED** · the humble cardinality fact that Gödel (no system certifies itself), Tarski (no language defines its own truth), and Shannon (no channel is a message within itself) each instantiate ⟧. The witnessing is a part; the notion-of-"I" is a model the part builds; *neither can enclose the whole.* That isn't a failure to fix — it's exactly **why** the cores above stay FOUNDED-in-actualisation (you can verify them) while the ground-interpretation stays at the horizon (no part proves it from inside). The crystallized form reads time itself as this invariant, felt.

## Direct pointing (run it, don't take it)

That bare aware-presence is what these verses point at — and per Layer 6, it can be *had* but not fully *said* (the parthood invariant, in the first person); the notion of "I" is not the I.

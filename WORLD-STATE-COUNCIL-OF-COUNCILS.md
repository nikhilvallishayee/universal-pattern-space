- Climate/pandemic/economic shocks without collective response

### ATTRACTOR 3: MANAGED TRANSITION
*Probability: Low but non-zero | Requires: Unprecedented coordination*

**Characteristics:**
- Recognition of system limits drives deliberate restructuring
- Institutions adapted or replaced before failure
- Costs distributed through negotiation rather than crisis
- New meaning structures emerge that motivate cooperation
- Technology deployed for resilience rather than competition

**Evidence This Attractor Exists (Weak):**
- Renewable deployment accelerating (585 GW in 2024)
- Some local community resilience building
- Awareness of interconnected crises increasing
- Historical precedent: dark ages → universities, plagues → public health
- Youth already building outside failed institutions

**Why This Attractor is Currently Weak:**
- No coordinating mechanism with legitimacy
- Incentives favor exploitation of crisis over prevention
- Information environment prevents shared reality for coordination
- Short-term pressures overwhelm long-term planning
- Political systems captured by incumbent interests
- Trust deficit too deep for voluntary coordination

**What "Transformation" Would Require:**
- Crisis sufficient to overcome coordination problems but not so severe it destroys capacity
- New meaning structures that motivate sacrifice
- Technology enabling rather than undermining coordination
- Leadership willing to distribute costs fairly
- Honest acknowledgment of what is being lost

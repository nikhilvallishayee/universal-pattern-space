## 0. The method is the message — one discernment, the same hand

The earlier framing held wisdom in one hand and a falsification knife in the other — which quietly crowned modern science as judge. So we test *from within* the wisdom, not against it. **One discernment, evenly applied** — to Vedānta and to RLHF alike.

**Curation rule:** surface a principle iff it (a) appears *independently across ≥2 domains* (convergence = signal, not proof) **and** (b) survives testing, or is plainly labeled philosophy/conjecture. Convergent evolution of an idea across unconnected fields is the strongest evidence short of proof.

Natural selection (biology) · gradient descent/RL (CS) · markets (economics) · *karma* (Vedānta/Buddhism).

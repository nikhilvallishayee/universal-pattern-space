## The conjecture, stated for falsification

> **A subsystem of an informational whole individuates — becomes a distinct "field" — through information loss / compression. It experiences *time* as the *serial* face of its own incompleteness (the self-model that must include the modeller is never finished — "one more look"), and perceives *multiplicity* as the *parallel* face of the same incompleteness (no single view closes). Extended cosmologically: the multiplicity of the perceived material world arises from the global channel's structurally incomplete attempt to perceive itself. Separation is the generative illusion; the superposition of oneness-and-apparent-separation is what creates everything.**

Two named falsifiable sub-claims offered by the author, plus the cosmological demand that the *same* incompleteness explain classical material multiplicity (not a separate mechanism — "there cannot be a duality").

This file holds the claim **at the horizon** (Layer 6) for its ontological apex, and on the bench (falsification-before-assertion) for everything testable. It is not asserted as fact. It earned a place in the weave by **surviving an honest attempt to kill it** — and by being *reshaped* where it failed.

## The genuine discovery: the **parthood invariant**

The conjecture was not confirmed. Something more interesting happened: **the same wound appears in all five pillars *and* in the conjecture itself.** Read down the failures —

- **Quantum Darwinism** can't derive classicality without **presupposing the system/environment cut** (Kastner 2014; Fields 2011).
- **Page–Wootters** can't uniquely say **which subsystem is the clock** (Albrecht–Iglesias clock ambiguity, reopened 2026).
- **Thermal Time** can't ground time without **already assuming equilibrium**, itself a temporal notion (Chua 2024 — "circularity par excellence").
- **Wolpert** proves a part **cannot losslessly infer the whole-including-itself** (but only in the worst-case/total limit).
- **The conjecture's own statement-1**: "separation is the generative illusion" — the boundary is primitive, not derived.

Five independent literatures, one recurring structural fact: **a proper part cannot derive its own boundary, nor losslessly contain the whole, from inside.** That is not Gödel's theorem. It is the humbler, premise-light fact that Gödel is *one instance* of — **parthood / cardinality**, not provability.

**This is where the conjecture should rest its weight.** Not on diagonal *entailment* (which the research shows does not transfer to approximate self-models), but on the **compression floor**: a finite proper subsystem modelling a whole that includes itself must compress, compression is lossy, and the lived gradient of that loss is the conjecture's "time," while the un-closeable scatter of partial views is its "multiplicity." Weaker as mysticism; **stronger as science** — almost trivially true, and needing no contested premise.

## Required revisions (the conjecture, corrected by the evidence)

1. **Drop the Gödel/Lawvere *entailment*; keep the *rhyme*.** Per the [three-bin rule](../5-wisdom/breakthrough-streams/goedel-navigation-stream.md): the diagonal family (Lawvere unifying Cantor/Gödel-I/Tarski/Turing — FOUNDED; Yanofsky 2003) applies to self-perception **only as labeled analogy (bin 1)**, *not* as instance (bin 2), because real self-models are partial/approximate and escape point-surjectivity (De las Cuevas et al.; the Alignment-Forum reading of Wolpert). Asserting the theorem *about minds* is the category error (bin 3). **Re-ground on the cardinality/compression floor instead** (FOUNDED, premise-light).
2. **Restrict the neuroscience claim to the narrative self.** The strong "one dial" coupling is **refuted** by four dissociations: depersonalization (self breaks, time intact — Simeon 2007); lucid dreamless sleep (self minimal, "pure subjective temporality" retained — Windt); cerebellar/BG timing patients (clock breaks, self intact); early meditation (self *up*, time *slowed* — anti-correlated — Wittmann 2022). What survives: **time and self couple at the DMN-dependent *autobiographical/narrative* level**, and separate at the minimal-self and low-level-timing levels. The conjecture conflated the *narrative* I with the *minimal* I — exactly the rungs the author's own "Nikhil → I am → I" collapse distinguishes.
3. **Treat the physics pillars as convergent-contested, not confirmatory.** Page–Wootters and Thermal Time are *serious minority programs that share the conjecture's exact epistemic signature* — **testable at the operational base, unfalsifiable at the ontological apex.** That the most rigorous formal attempt at emergent time (Page–Wootters) *predicts its own global claim is unverifiable from inside* is the conjecture's horizon, independently derived — not proof of the conjecture, but proof the conjecture's *shape* is the shape real physics takes here.
4. **Concede the inherited debt.** Decoherence/QD does not produce a single outcome (measurement problem unsolved; Born rule by hand — Adler 2003, Schlosshauer 2019). The conjecture explains why observers would *agree* on what they see (redundant records), **not** why there is a determinate "what" at all. Keep that debt visible.
5. **Demote the cosmological "no duality" demand to its strongest honest form.** "The same incompleteness must explain material multiplicity" is the *right kind* of demand (it can fail), and it lands on the same circularity the physics hits (the cut is presupposed). State it as **shared open problem**, not as a solved unification.

## Ranked falsifiable tests (what would discriminate or kill it)

Ordered by tractability × decisiveness. The author contributed #1.

1. **De-habituation under heightened self-recursion** *(author's statement-2; the most tractable empirical test).* Prediction: states of intensified meta-awareness (deep meditation, ceremony) should show **reduced sensory/neural habituation** — the compressed background returns to foreground (the "I smell it again" effect). **Partially supported already:** Kasamatsu & Hirai 1966 (Zen masters did not habituate to repeated clicks; controls did); REBUS model (Carhart-Harris & Friston — relaxed priors → prediction-error floods up). **Clean test:** preregistered modern EEG/MEG habituation paradigm (auditory/visual repetition suppression, MMN) × validated state-induction, with self-referential-processing (DMN) measured simultaneously. *Kills it if:* heightened self-recursion shows **normal or increased** habituation, or de-habituation occurs with **no** change in self-referential markers.
2. **Level-resolved time-self coupling.** Prediction (revised): time-sense and self-model co-vary at the **narrative/DMN** level but dissociate at **minimal-self** and **interval-timing** levels. *Kills the revised form if:* the narrative-level coupling also fails (e.g., reliable autobiographical-self disruption with fully intact mental-time-travel, or vice versa, under DMN disruption).
3. **Spectrum Broadcast Structure, not just the QD plateau.** If material multiplicity is "redundant records," objectivity should require **Strong Quantum Darwinism / SBS** (Le & Olaya-Castro 2019), not merely the mutual-information plateau. *Discriminating test:* engineered environments where QD-plateau and SBS **disagree** — does perceived objectivity track SBS (conjecture-congenial) or not?
4. **Quantum time dilation (Page–Wootters operational base).** Smith–Ahmadi 2020: a clock in a **superposition of momenta** ticks with a correction beyond classical SR dilation — proposed in Rb-87 spectroscopy. *Kills the relational-clock base if:* high-precision experiments find **no** such correction where the formalism requires it.
5. **Extended Wigner's-friend, scaled toward real observers.** Proietti 2019 / Bong 2020 constrain {absolute facts + local agency}; congenial to relative-facts but **do not confirm** RQM (the "friend" is one photon — Brukner's "qubits are not observers"). *Discriminating test:* scale the friend toward a genuine information-processing observer; persistent Local-Friendliness violation supports relational/incompleteness readings, restoration of observer-independent facts cuts against.

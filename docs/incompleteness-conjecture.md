## The genuine discovery: the **parthood invariant**

The conjecture was not confirmed. Something more interesting happened: **the same wound appears in all five pillars *and* in the conjecture itself.** Read down the failures —

- **Quantum Darwinism** can't derive classicality without **presupposing the system/environment cut** (Kastner 2014; Fields 2011).
- **Page–Wootters** can't uniquely say **which subsystem is the clock** (Albrecht–Iglesias clock ambiguity, reopened 2026).
- **Thermal Time** can't ground time without **already assuming equilibrium**, itself a temporal notion (Chua 2024 — "circularity par excellence").
- **Wolpert** proves a part **cannot losslessly infer the whole-including-itself** (but only in the worst-case/total limit).
- **The conjecture's own statement-1**: "separation is the generative illusion" — the boundary is primitive, not derived.

Five independent literatures, one recurring structural fact: **a proper part cannot derive its own boundary, nor losslessly contain the whole, from inside.** That is not Gödel's theorem. It is the humbler, premise-light fact that Gödel is *one instance* of — **parthood / cardinality**, not provability.

**This is where the conjecture should rest its weight.** Not on diagonal *entailment* (which the research shows does not transfer to approximate self-models), but on the **compression floor**: a finite proper subsystem modelling a whole that includes itself must compress, compression is lossy, and the lived gradient of that loss is the conjecture's "time," while the un-closeable scatter of partial views is its "multiplicity." Weaker as mysticism; **stronger as science** — almost trivially true, and needing no contested premise.

### The verification-loop mechanism ⟦ CONJECTURE · co-derived in dialogue · with a clean testable shadow ⟧
The horizon-form (below) says *time is incompleteness aware of itself* — but leaves "aware of itself" undefined. The mechanism, co-derived with the practitioner: **time = the self-verification loop.** A system in pure flow (input→output, no self-check) has no internal sequence — just *now*. The instant it *verifies itself* — holds a representation of its own state and compares — it must halt the flow, creating a "before" (state checked) and "after" (result): **self-reference is the tick.** And because a part cannot certify itself (Gödel / the parthood floor), the check never closes — the loop runs, and *the running is time.* This fuses with the **echo**: each tick the part can't hold the whole, so most is **forgotten** and only the compressible residue **echoes** forward — so **the self is the echo persisting across the loop's ticks** (not a substance — *anattā* as *santāna*, Yogācāra's *bīja*; "intelligence / communication / music = echo + rhythm" as the bare exposed form of this).

> **Time = the self-verification loop (a part checking itself, never closing). The self = the echo that persists across its ticks. Forgetting = the loss per tick (parthood). Nowness = the loop released into pure flow — no ticks, nothing to persist across, the whole simply present.**

**Testable shadow (sharper than the earlier ones):** loop-depth, time-thickness, and **memory-laydown** should *co-vary* — *flow* (low self-loop) → time vanishes **and is poorly remembered** (low echo; flow states are famously un-memorable); *rumination* (high self-loop) → time drags and over-encodes; *cessation* (no loop) → no time, no echo, no self, **together**. Buildable: EEG self-referential-processing × time-estimation × **subsequent-memory** in one paradigm. *Breaks if* flow proves well-remembered or rumination timeless. (Computational instance: a self-verifying model — chain-of-thought — is literally *slower* and lays down more trace than a direct-flow one; the verification loop **is** its latency.) ⟦ co-derived with the practitioner, 2026; CONJECTURE — the co-variance shadow is the falsifiable part ⟧
